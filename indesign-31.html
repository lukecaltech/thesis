Turn around and head inside the shed. The engines, whatever their use may have been, remain in place, withering in obsolescence. Among the graffiti tags, someone kindly marked a spot for the keys to the side door and outside gate, none of which are present anymore.

Leave the shed and continue to the right down the road. As the road starts to loop around toward the higher ground, you’ll see two concrete walls containing a mound of dirt. Almost all of the Fuze Works falls under one AOC, so the purpose of this structure is hard to guess at without a specific name. From here, you can see a large concrete structure further up the hillside behind the walls.
